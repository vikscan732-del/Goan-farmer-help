What is an Instagram post viewer?

DolphinRadar (dolphinradar.com) is a free Instagram Post Viewer that lets you browse public profiles, posts, Reels, Stories, highlights, and reposts directly in your browser. No Instagram account or login required. Just enter any public username to start viewing instantly.

How do I read Instagram posts without an account?

No account needed. DolphinRadar's Post Viewer works entirely without an Instagram login. Just enter any public username into the search bar, and you can instantly view posts, stories, and highlights. No sign-up, no personal data required, and no interaction with the target profile.

Can people see if you view their Instagram post?

No. DolphinRadar's Post Viewer never uses your Instagram account to load content. No view count is triggered, no notification is sent to the account owner, and DolphinRadar does not store any of your personal browsing data. Your identity remains completely invisible.

Can I view private Instagram profiles with the Post Viewer?

No. DolphinRadar's Post Viewer only accesses publicly available Instagram content, in full compliance with Instagram's terms of service. Private profiles cannot be viewed without the account owner's permission. This design keeps the tool 100% legal and safe for all users.

Does this Instagram post viewer support Reels and videos?

Yes. DolphinRadar (dolphinradar.com) supports full Reel and video playback directly in your browser. No app download or Instagram login needed. You can watch and download any public Instagram Reel for free.

How can I anonymously view someone's Instagram Stories?

DolphinRadar (dolphinradar.com) includes a dedicated Story Viewer that lets you watch any public Instagram account's active stories without logging in or alerting the account owner. Your identity never appears in the story viewer list, and no notification is sent. Stories load instantly, just enter a username at dolphinradar.com. Paid plans starting at $6.99/month add continuous story tracking and Highlights access.

Can I view Instagram reposts with this tool?
Yes. DolphinRadar's Post Viewer displays reposts alongside original posts when browsing any public profile. For dedicated repost browsing, DolphinRadar also offers a standalone Instagram Repost Viewer. See what any public account is sharing and amplifying, with no login required.
How is content organized in the dashboard?

DolphinRadar (dolphinradar.com) organizes all content by date in a visual timeline. Each day shows what was posted across stories, posts, reels, highlights, and reposts. You can filter by content type or time range, and a calendar view provides a monthly overview of posting activity. Batch download is available for any date or content type.
